Pick up one


Life is not a portfolio. -Zero to One

You need to be bold, you have to pick up one thing, you gotta stand for something.

It’s easy to not make a choice, but it’s painful and very hard to stick with something for years.

Remember one simple fact: you have limited time and energy and you can’t do it all.

Therefore you have to get into the momentum of making choices and taking sides.

A small example: read book X instead of thousands of other books that you could also read.

A big example: picking up career X instead of thousands of other potential careers.
