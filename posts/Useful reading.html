Useful reading


Reading is great but you need to make effort to gain the fruits of reading.

Just reading a book, then ignore it for the rest of your life, sure have some benefits but not that much.

So what’s the solution?

Don’t abandon your books after reading them!

Use books as a reference.

Keep your books, and keep going back to them.

Whenever you encounter a problem there’s a book you’ve read that might contain a solution.

Visit a book you have read from time to time, like an old friend, to find something useful you can apply into your life.

Let’s imagine you are a writer, and you have read a great book about writing, after reading it, you can go back to it whenever you encounter a writing problem, use it to enhance your next piece of writing, take new ideas and apply them, … etc.

You need to use books not only read them.

Deploy what you read into the process of what you do and in the process of life in general, in all aspects.
