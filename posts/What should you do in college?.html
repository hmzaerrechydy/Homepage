What should you do in college?


My first year in college is about to finish, I will describe my experience so far in one word: Chaos!

It was a storm of problems and endless questions about everything: studying, time management, learning new skills, gaining experience, building a business, …

After all of this I can say that I have a clear vision of what a college student should do in this period of 3 to 4 years.

I have learned that I should do two things: learning and networking.

Learning: develop a body of knowledge, acquire new skills and gain experience. To do that there’s endless ways to do so, read books, take online MOOCS in different fields, build stuff like a startup or an open source for example. Why? After college you will need this skills, knowledge and experience to launch your career in a world of hyper competition.

Networking: you need to make new relationships with people that can help you to learn and attract opportunities to your way, you can do this offline in college, events, … also online trough social media, even blogging can be considered a way to network and get to know new people who will stumble across your blog posts. This is also will make the launch of your career after graduation easier.

This blog post is for me to clarify my vision for the rest of college.
