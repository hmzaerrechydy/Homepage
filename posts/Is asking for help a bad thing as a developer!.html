Is asking for help a bad thing as a developer!


Learn to ask for help.

When building a project, working on an assignment, or solving a problem you will get stuck somewhere on the road after trying so hard to find a solution and write your own code.

What to do?

Work even harder and don’t look for the hints, no!

You will end up only wasting time, or giving up.

Asking for help is not a weakness in programming but a skill that you need to develop and master.

The most brilliant programmers still ask questions and learn from their peers.

After trying so hard go look up help, learn, apply what you’ve learned and move to the next problem, assignment or project.
