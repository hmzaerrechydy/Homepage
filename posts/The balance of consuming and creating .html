The balance of consuming and creating


You need to keep balance between this two things: consuming and creating.

Reading books, blogs, listening to podcasts, watching videos and tutorials, taking a MOOC, … all of this is consuming.

Writing, coding, design, video editing, filming, … that’s creating.

Consume to get information, find ideas, learn new techniques, and absorb the wisdom of others.

Create to learn, acquire skills, build knowledge, expertise, apply what you’ve consumed and develop your own wisdom.

Divide your time between the two activities, 90% for creating and 10% for consuming is a good formula.
