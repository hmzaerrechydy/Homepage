The perfect daily routine


I finished the new daily routine experiment!

Read this blog post to learn more about this experiment. [LINK]

The previous week I decided to be productive only during the mornings and spend the rest of the day resting and doing whatever I like.

I spent 7 days living that way and here’s what I’ve learned:

First thing this routine is obviously perfectly balanced, I had a huge window to rest and enjoy my time without any burnout.

It kept me focused, because I had a small period of time during the day where I need to be productive that pushed me to focus and stay on track to finish as much work as I can without any procrastination.

One obvious down side is I had less time to do what I want to do.

The experiment is done but I will not quit this daily routine forever, this routine is great for days where I want to have more free time for whatever reason.

For example if I’m physically, mentally or emotionally tired then I can switch this easy routine to make sure I make progress and at the same time give myself more time to rest.
