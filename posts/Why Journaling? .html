Why Journaling?


Journaling helps you make decisions you might not make, you slow down and make room for novel thoughts.

Passages I liked from the book (Rework) I was reading today :

When you don’t know what you believe, everything becomes an argument. Everything is debatable. But when you stand for something, decisions are obvious.

Raising money is incredibly distracting.

Great companies start in garages all the time.

Do you really need X can’t you do Y? This is a great mental exercise to always to question X which is the expensive option and figure out Y the cheaper, simpler, easier and affordable path. (I learned this from Rework)

Reading a book is a starting point to discover ideas that don’t exist in the book nor your mind.
