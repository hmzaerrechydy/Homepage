Reading blogs


Recently I started reading blogs.

I read books already and I wanted to experiment with a different medium.

Blogs is a source of unique ideas.

You can learn about topics that you can’t find in books.

And see people’s experiences, a documentation of their process learning code, building a startup, …

You will know new people, it’s a great networking method, a blog will lead you to other blogs, trough links, it’s a rabbit hole, a great one.
