Micro vs Macro


Small is a great destination in itself. —Rework by David Heinemeier Hansson and Jason Fried

You can turn macro projects into small easy and simple to mange efficient micro components, like the internet, it is not a big software that lives inside one developer’s computer with millions of lines of code, instead it the result of contribution of many individuals, developers, companies, organizations …

If all you do is work, you're unlikely to have sound judgments. —Rework

On the contrast if you have fewer hours you will focus only on doing the most important work.
