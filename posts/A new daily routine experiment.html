A new daily routine experiment


This week I’m running an experiment: a new daily routine.

My current daily routine is to spend the morning and afternoon doing only productive things: studying, reading, exercise, journaling, blogging, … and I chill and relax in the evening and sleep in the night.

This week I want to give more time to rest, in the morning I will be as productive as I can, and spend the rest of the day resting and do whatever I like.

Morning = Productivity(studying, exercise, writing, …)

Afternoon + Evening = Rest, chill and entertainment

Night = Sleep

Why I’m doing this? I don’t believe in weekends, vacations and retirement, instead of burning out now and promise myself some rest at the end of the week or in summer I prefer instead to bring balance to my days, my current routine is great at achieving that but I wondered if I could give myself even more rest and still be productive or be more productive and increase my energy and focus.

Note: I will still do things I have to do, non-optional work, during the rest period of my day if there’s any.

It’s Monday, the first day of the experiment.

On Sunday I will share the results, insights, lessons, failures … and anything I learned from this experience and what my daily routine will look like whether I will go back to my current routine, adopt this new routine or have a mix between them.
