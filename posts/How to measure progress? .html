How to measure progress?


Progress is measured by setting and accomplishing objectives.

If you don’t know where you want to go then you will never know when you get there! no amount of work will satisfy you, not because you didn’t work hard enough it’s just because you didn’t define what good work is, no one will tell, it’s your job to define the standards and objectives, before doing any work always define what “good” is so you know it when you achieve it.
