How to stick to a habit?


Don’t do it everyday!

Why?

People who exercise have rest days, they don’t exercise on this days to recover and rest.

Apply the same mindset to other habits.

Rest days from your habit to rest not just physically but mentally, to avoid burn out, and keep motivation for what you do.

Of course there’s exceptions for this rule, if a habit is easy for you then you can do it every day, for me some of this daily habits are: reading, journaling, blogging, …
